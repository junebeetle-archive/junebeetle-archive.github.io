Maze Extended

A couple years ago, when I was learning Processing, I made a simple platformer engine. And since so many people liked my Scratch Maze, I decided to make an improved version: Maze Extended. Now with scrolling, slightly less boring music, and wayyyy more detailed instructions. Oh, and also a 3x larger map! To the point that beating it is, well, kind of tedious. You know what, just download it anyways.
